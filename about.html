About Your Info

What is Your Info?

Your Info is a comprehensive personal portfolio platform designed to help you showcase your achievements, manage your professional profile, and keep track of important events in your life. Whether you're a professional looking to create an online presence or someone who wants to organize their accomplishments, Your Info provides all the tools you need.

Key Features

Personal Profile

Create a comprehensive profile with your professional information, skills, education, and experience. Upload a profile photo and customize your information to showcase who you are.

Achievement Tracking

Document and showcase your achievements, certifications, awards, and milestones. Organize them by category and date to create a comprehensive record of your accomplishments.

Calendar & Events

Stay organized with an integrated calendar system. Track upcoming events, set reminders, and mark events as completed. Never miss an important date again.

Dashboard Overview

Get a quick overview of your upcoming events, recent achievements, and important statistics all in one place. Your personalized dashboard keeps you informed at a glance.
